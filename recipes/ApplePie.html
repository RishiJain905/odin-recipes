Description

Apple pie is a classic dessert made with a pastry crust filled with sliced apples, sugar, and spices such as cinnamon and nutmeg. It is baked until the crush is golden and the apples are tender. Often enjoyed on its own or with a sccop of ice cream.

  • All Purpose Flour
  • sugar
  • ground cinnamon
  • nutmeg
  • lemon juice
  • egg

Steps

  1. mix dry ingredients in a bowl with salt and sugar
  2. cut in butter
  3. add water and gradually mix
  4. Form the dough by shaping it
  5. prep the apples and slice them
  6. roll out the dough
  7. add the filling
  8. bake for 45-50 minutes at 425 F
  9. cool then serve
