Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular italian dish made of a round, flat base leavened wheat based dough topped with tomatoies, cheese, and othen various other ingredients. It is baked at a high tempreture, traditionally in a wood-fired oven, and it is typically cut into slices for serving. pizza is enjoyed worldwide in many different styles

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Dough prep: Mix ingredients with dry yeast and water. Make the dough, then knead the dough and let it rise
  2. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F
  3. Prepare the dough by rolling it out into a round shape
  4. Add the sauce, a think layer
  5. Add cheese and any other toppings of your choice
